- Meet Yara

Yara is your A.I. wealth manager at the palm of your hand.

- Our Platform

Yara is basically Siri meets Fidelity Investments. Yara is an automatic wealth management system that you can speak or type to manage your portfolio and is available 24/7 via web or mobile. Just speak or type to Yara. As you work and play, Yara is constantly monitoring changes in your portfolio and if things change in your investment profile or portfolio, it will automically make those changes to keep you on track.

- What our System Does

Wealth Management

Yara will constantly monitor your portfolio and make improvements.

Trading

Through voice and text, you can tell Yara to trade for you (e.g. "Buy 10 shares of Apple").

Analysis

Get real-time analysis of your portfolio sent to your mobile device.

Machine Learning

Yara is a system that will learn your portfolio and make decisions tailored for you.

Voice & Text Recognition

Interact with Yara through text or just simply speaking into your microphone.

24/7 Availability

Yara is available and working 24/7 to answer questions and to monitor your portfolio.
