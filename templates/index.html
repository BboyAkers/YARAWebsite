- Meet Yara

Yara is your A.I. wealth manager at the palm of your hand.

YARA stands for Your Asset & Risk Automation. Yara is your virtual financial manager for all of your investments. You can text it, speak to it, and have it use artificial intelligence to automatically empower you to better manage your investments.

- Our Platform

Yara is able to analyze your entire portfolio, provide unbiased recommendations for improvements, and learn from your preferred investment style to source opportunities accordingly. You can finally sleep well at night feeling like you’ve got a smart system constantly monitoring your portfolio and working for you and your monty 24/7 (Yara never sleeps).
