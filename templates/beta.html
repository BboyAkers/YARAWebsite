- Management

While you work and play, let Yara manage your portfolio for less fees. Some of the features are highlighted below. More features coming soon!

  • Yara will learn your portfolio and make decisions tailored for you.
  • Yara will constantly monitor your portfolio and make improvements.
  • You can either tell Yara to rebalance your portfolio or let Yara rebalance for you.
  • Yara can analyze and let you know how to diversify your portfolio.
  • Yara can optimize your portfolio by showing you how much to invest in each of your investments to give you the best return to risk.
